Caramelized Pork Belly Soup

Description

Kaw Sach Chrouk (Caramelized Pork Belly soup) has been my favorite Cambodian soup ever since I was a child. It's both sweet and savoury AND the boiled eggs are a must have in this dish.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Sweet, dark soy sauce
  • Light soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Ground black pepper
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Eggs, as many as you wish
  • Rice

Steps

  1. First wash your rice several times before placing it in a rice cooker or pot. While rice is cooking, boil your eggs as you prepare the caramel base.
  2. Caramelize the sugar over medium-low heat until brown.
  3. Lower the heat and add in the fish sauce and soy sauces. Stir until sugar and sauces are combined.
  4. Turn the heat back to medium-high and add in the pork. Mix well and stir fry until meat turns brown.
  5. Once the meat is cooked, pour enough water into the pot to just barely cover the meat. Add in your hard boiled eggs and place a lid on the pot and let it come to a boil before turning down the heat and letting it simmer for xx minutes.
  6. Once finished, serve with a bowl of warm rice.
