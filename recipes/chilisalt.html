Spicy Chili Salt

Description

A salty and spicy mixture perfect for enjoying sour mangoes or pineapple. This is a popular mouthwatering treat found in Southeast Asia!

For this recipe please use a mortar and pestle.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Red Thai chili peppers (stems taken off)
  • 4 tbsp Salt
  • Pinch of Tamarind Soup Mix (optional)

Steps

  1. If you haven't already, remove the stems from the chili peppers.
  2. Combine salt, Thai peppers, and Tamarind Soup Mix into the mortar.
  3. Using the pestle, grind together all ingredients until salt turns pink or red or until the peppers are squished to your desire.
  4. Grab your favorite sour fruit and enjoy!
