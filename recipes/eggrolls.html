Cambodian Eggrolls

Description

A classic appetizer that is a must have! These meat filled deep fried egg rolls are flavorful and easy to eat nonstop!

Ingredients

Makes roughly 20 eggrolls
  • pack of eggroll wrappers
  • 1 egg, yolk is optional
  • 100g ground pork
  • 1 medium carrot, shredded
  • 1 small to medium taro, shredded
  • small amount of Vietnamese glass noodles, boiled and cut into small pieces
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • minced garlic
  • fish sauce
  • chicken soup powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  • im asian ingredients gotta be measured by the eyes im sorry

Steps

  1. Set aside the wrappers and 1 egg
  2. Combine all remaining ingredients into a large mixing bowl. Season to your liking and mix well.
  3. Crack the 1 egg into a small bowl and mix, this will be used to seal the wrapper.
  4. Open the package of wrappers and carefully peel off one wrap. Place the wrapper in a diamond shape. Put a spoonful of filling on the end closest to you and begin to roll, remember to fold in the corners so no oil gets in.
  5. Dip your finger into the beaten egg and drip it onto the last corner of the wrapper to roll and seal it shut.
  6. Continue until all meat fillings or wrappers are finished.
  7. Deep fry those delicious snacks until golden brown
